Getting to DC

The closest airport is Reagan National Airport (DCA), about 15 minutes from our venue. Dulles (IAD) and Baltimore (BWI) are also options, about 45-60 minutes away.

Getting to The Ceremony and Reception

For those in the DMV area, we recommend taking Metro or Lyft/Uber. The venue is less than a 5-minute walk from the Mt Vernon Sq 7th St-Convention Center stop.

If you'd rather drive, there is limited street parking in the area, though there are paid parking lots in the area.

Hotels

There are plenty of hotel options within a 10-minute walk from the venue. There's no official hotel, but we have a few recommendations:

  • Marriott Marquis
  • Kimpton Hotel Monaco
  • Homewood Suites DC Convention Center
